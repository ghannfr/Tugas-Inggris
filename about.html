Our About Us

UKRI LEARNING is an education program dedicated to providing high quality education for our students. We believe that every child has the potential to succeed and we strive to provide a supportive and inspiring learning environment. Established in 1990, UKRI LEARNING has evolved from a simple program to a well-established and ready-to-use education program. We continue to innovate to meet all the educational needs of our students.


Our Mission & Vision

Vision

To become an independent, innovative, and superior learning and research university at the national level and become one of the centers for the development of civilization and national resilience.

Mission

  • Organizing higher education in the fields of science, technology, arts and humanities that produce graduates who have excellence in their scientific fields, are independent, open, by prioritizing the application of science for the welfare of the nation.
  • Organizing innovative basic and applied research / research, and producing works that will support the empowerment and improvement of the welfare and resilience of the Indonesian nation.
  • Organizing integrated community service from the academic community by applying scientific-based solutions to problems that exist in society.
